Computers

Computer Hardware & Software

The first computer that me and my family had was a Compaq Presario 7000 PC that originally ran Windows Me (Millenium Edition, the last 9x-based OS from Microsoft’s Windows line of operating systems that was known for its infamous OS instability and rushed release in 2000) before I was born, which was presumably upgraded to the NT-based Windows XP around 2001 when it was released to the general public in October of that year. With an Intel Pentium 4 processor, a Nvidia-based graphics card, a Sound Blaster-based sound card, a 320 GB hard disk drive and with 4 MB of memory, this computer served well throughout the mid-2000’s, until it was replaced with an HP Pavilion media center computer running Windows Vista from 2008 onwards.
