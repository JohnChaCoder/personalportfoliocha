My name is Johnny Cha. I was a senior at Clovis East High School and in The Center for Advanced Research and Technology (CART), enrolled in classes that I am interested in taking. In that class, I have tried my best to put in the best effort that I can to my advantage, without being distracted or side-tracked by other things.
